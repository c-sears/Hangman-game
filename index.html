Welcome to my game of Hangman!

    Some basic rules to get you started:

  • Select your letter by simply pressing it on the keyboard
  • Watch out! You have 100hp at the beginning of each new word, but every wrong letter drops it by 10hp!
  • Once you die your total score will be reset, but you are more than welcome to keep playing!

I hope you have as much fun playing this game as I did making it!!!
